Lecture 6: Mathematical Induction

1. Principle of Mathematical Induction

A technique to prove a propositional function P(n) is true for all positive integers n. It operates like an infinite ladder.

  • Basis Step: Verify that P(1) is true.
  • Inductive Step: Assume P(k) is true (Inductive Hypothesis), and prove that P(k+1) must also be true.
