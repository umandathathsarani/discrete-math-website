Lecture 3: Sequences & Summations

1. Sequences

An ordered list of elements where duplicates are allowed and order matters.

  • Arithmetic Sequence: Increases by a constant difference (d). Formula: an = a + (n-1)d
  • Geometric Sequence: Increases by a constant factor (common ratio, r). Formula: un = arn-1

2. Summations (Σ)

Sigma notation concisely represents the sum of a sequence of numbers.

Σ (from j=m to n) aj = am + am+1 + ... + an

Formulas: Sum of a geometric progression: Sn = a(1-rn) / (1-r) where r ≠ 1.
