📜 Master Practice Bank (20 Questions)

Q1

What is the difference between a permutation and a combination?

🪄 Answer

In combinations, order does not matter.

Q2

Calculate C(5, 2).

🪄 Answer

10.

Q3

What is the formula for combinations without repetition?

🪄 Answer

C(n, r) = n! / (r!(n-r)!).

Q4

What is C(n, n)?

🪄 Answer

1.

Q5

What is C(n, 0)?

🪄 Answer

1.

Q6

Calculate C(10, 1).

🪄 Answer

10.

Q7

What is C(n, 1)?

🪄 Answer

n.

Q8

Does order matter in combinations?

🪄 Answer

No.

Q9

What defines the Binomial Theorem?

🪄 Answer

A way to expand (x + y)n.

Q10

In (x+y)n, how many terms are in the expansion?

🪄 Answer

n + 1.

Q11

What is row 0 of Pascal's triangle?

🪄 Answer

1.

Q12

🪄 Answer

Q13

🪄 Answer

6.

Q14

What is the sum of entries in row n of Pascal's Triangle?

🪄 Answer

2n.

Q15

What is C(n, r) equal to in terms of symmetry?

🪄 Answer

C(n, n-r).

Q16

Calculate C(6, 3).

🪄 Answer

20.

Q17

If items are repeated, which formula applies?

🪄 Answer

C(n+r-1, r).

Q18

🪄 Answer

True.

Q19

🪄 Answer

Q20

If n=3, what is the binomial expansion of (x+y)3?

🪄 Answer
